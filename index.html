ABOUT ME

Aspiring Web Designer / Developer

I have always been passionate about learning ..... if it interests me I have to know all of it. That was what drew me to the financial markets as I liked the idea of doing something where things would always change and there would always be new things to learn and consider. I went to the commodites exchange in NYC and had a successful career as a derivatives trader where I only got paid if I got it right....no clients, no salary, no support other than my own team which I ended up managing to carry out my market opinions trading multiple products at once. It's a difficult industry where less than 1 in 20 make it past the first year and I was able to be profitable every year for over a decade...... then things changed. The financial markets started changing the rules and you could be "wrong" even though you had the right ideas and "right" for the wrong reasons and the exchanges all shut down. So with the industry changing I chose to do something different where I would be able to build tangible projects with real world application. To this end I've found my way to the Full Stack Software Development Bootcamp at the University of Pennsylvania where I am currently engaged full time in learning all about as many aspects of software development as possible in 6 months. I know that my ability to work quickly and efficiently without errors and my creativity and problem solving skills will make me to be a strong asset to any development team once I have the core competencies in place and am building on them every day. I am soley focused on this endeavor now and looking to work for a company where I believe in what they do and are making a positive impact on people's lives.
